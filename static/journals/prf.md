# Physical Review Fluids leaning

Frame the work around a general physical, modeling, or computational question in fluid dynamics.

- Keep motivation compact and move quickly to the question, parameters, and approach.
- Emphasize mechanisms, regimes, scaling, and limitations over engineering promotion.
- For perspectives or historical reviews, disclose the organizing lens and distinguish personal interpretation from established history.
- Use journal formatting conventions only when requested; prioritize rhetorical fit over mechanically copying Roman-numeral headings.
