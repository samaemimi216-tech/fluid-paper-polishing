# Title

Name the phenomenon or method and, when it changes interpretation, the regime, geometry, or application.

- Prefer an informative noun phrase or bounded finding over `A study of` and unsupported novelty claims.
- For method papers, expose the defining property rather than stacking generic praise words.
- For physical studies, lead with the phenomenon rather than the numerical tool unless the tool is the contribution.
- Keep abbreviations only when standard and essential for searchability.
- Verify that every claim in the title is supported across the tested parameter range.
