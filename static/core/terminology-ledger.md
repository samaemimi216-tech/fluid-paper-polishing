# Terminology and notation ledger

For a full section or manuscript, extract and lock recurring items before editing:

- method, model, solver, reconstruction, closure, and collision-model names;
- flow regimes, physical domains, phases, species, and boundary types;
- dimensional and nondimensional variables, units, subscripts, superscripts, and operators;
- abbreviations and their first-use expansions;
- reference, bulk, averaged, normalized, and perturbation quantities.

Use one canonical term for one concept. Do not introduce synonyms merely for variety. Flag a collision when one symbol names two quantities or one quantity changes symbols. Show a compact ledger only when inconsistency is present or the user requests a manuscript-wide revision.
