# Fluid-paper reader workflow

Help the reader answer these questions in order:

1. **Problem** - What flow or transport problem is being solved, and in which regime?
2. **Gap** - Which physical, modeling, numerical, or measurement limitation remains?
3. **Approach** - What was changed, coupled, derived, measured, or computed?
4. **Trust** - Which analysis, convergence evidence, benchmark, or experiment supports the claim?
5. **Insight** - What physical or computational understanding follows?
6. **Boundary** - Under which assumptions, geometries, and parameter ranges does it hold?

Do not bury the gap behind derivation detail or state broad implications before establishing trust.
