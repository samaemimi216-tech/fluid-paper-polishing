# Multiphase and reacting-flow conventions

- Identify phases/species, coupling direction, interphase exchange terms, reaction mechanism, and averaging framework.
- Distinguish Eulerian-Eulerian, Eulerian-Lagrangian, particle, wave-particle, interface-capturing, and interface-tracking descriptions.
- Define volume fraction, mass loading, Stokes number, particle Reynolds number, Damkohler number, equivalence ratio, and other controlling groups used.
- Separate phase velocity from mixture velocity and material density from bulk density.
- Tie claims about clustering, segregation, ignition, extinction, flame speed, or instability to diagnostics and operating conditions.
