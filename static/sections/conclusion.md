# Conclusion

Use `problem -> contribution -> strongest evidence -> physical/numerical insight -> limitations and scope`.

For comparison studies, close with the trade-off and regime-dependent choice. For reviews, close with a prioritized research agenda rather than a generic list of topics.

Do not repeat the abstract, introduce new results, or claim universal applicability from a finite benchmark set. State future work only when it follows from an identified limitation. Keep claimed regimes identical to those actually analyzed or tested.
